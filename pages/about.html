About The Histamine Handbook

The Histamine Handbook is a practical guide for people dealing with histamine intolerance or sensitivity. You get clear food lists, sensible recipes, supplement guidance, and sleep and stress strategies. The goal is simple: cut the confusion about what to eat and how to live so symptoms are calmer and life gets easier.

Do these sound familiar?

Histamine intolerance can cause chronic headaches, bloating, skin rashes, itchy eyes, a runny nose, palpitations, insomnia, and that annoying “wired but tired” feeling. Flare-ups look random until you spot the pattern. By tackling diet and lifestyle together, The Histamine Handbook gives you a plan that actually helps.
