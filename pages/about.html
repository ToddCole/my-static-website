About The Histamine handbook

The Histamine Handbook is a soon-to-be-released guide for anyone struggling with histamine intolerance or sensitivity. Packed with practical tools like recipes, food lists, supplement guidance and sleep strategies, it cuts through the confusion around what you can and can’t eat, and how lifestyle factors impact your symptoms.

Instead of vague advice, it gives clear, usable solutions you can apply immediately—whether that’s swapping out trigger foods, picking the right supplements, or making simple daily changes to improve recovery and energy. The book also shines a light on the often-misunderstood symptoms of histamine issues, which can mimic everything from allergies to anxiety. Think chronic headaches, bloating, skin rashes, itchy eyes, runny nose, irregular heartbeats, insomnia, and that frustrating “wired but tired” feeling. For many, these flare-ups can seem random or unrelated until histamine intolerance is identified as the common thread. By addressing these symptoms through diet and lifestyle, The Histamine Handbook offers a lifeline to people who’ve been brushed off as “just stressed” or “just sensitive” when in reality their body is overloaded with histamine.
