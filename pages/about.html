About The Histamine handbook

The Histamine Handbook is a soon-to-be-released guide for anyone struggling with histamine intolerance or sensitivity. Packed with practical tools like recipes, food lists, supplement guidance and sleep strategies, it cuts through the confusion around what you can and can’t eat, and how lifestyle factors impact your symptoms.

Do You Suffer From?

Histamine intolerance can casue chronic headaches, bloating, skin rashes, itchy eyes, runny nose, irregular heartbeats, insomnia, and that frustrating “wired but tired” feeling. For many, these flare-ups can seem random or unrelated until histamine intolerance is identified as the common thread. By addressing these symptoms through diet and lifestyle, The Histamine Handbook offers a lifeline to people who’ve been brushed off as “just stressed” or “just sensitive” when in reality their body is overloaded with histamine.

Person-Centered

Histamine affects everyone differently because our bodies process it at varying speeds and tolerances. Some can eat aged cheese or drink red wine without issue, while others get headaches, rashes, or stomach problems. Genetics, gut health, hormones, and stress all play a role. That’s why histamine intolerance looks different for each person—no two reactions are alike.
